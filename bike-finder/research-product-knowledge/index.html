Biking Experience

Where bikes are sold?

  • General Retail Store
  • Bike store:
    • Typically more reputable and will be able to have specialized advice, especially compared general retail stores. Furthermore, will might have more types of bikes and more likely to be in stock. However, will be typically more pricey.
  • Second-hand store

Online vs. Offline.

Offline you'll be able to get the bike that fits to you and your preferances. Furthermore, it's more likely you'd be able to get help in person, who might be able to adjust a bike. Howvever, online shopping has the general convenience of choosing a bike. If you are confident in your choice, this might be the better option.
